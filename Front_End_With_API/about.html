About Vijaya College

Vijaya College is a renowned government school in Sri Lanka with a strong academic tradition and a clear focus on developing students in science, mathematics, and technology. Our school fosters discipline, leadership, and excellence from Grades 6 to Advanced Level (A/L).

At Vijaya College, the Physical stream is one of our core strengths. We offer high-quality education in Combined Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and Information Technology providing students with a strong foundation for university-level studies in Engineering, Physical Sciences, ICT, and Architecture.

Our experienced mathematics teachers use modern, student-friendly techniques to guide learners toward mastering logical reasoning, problem-solving, and analytical thinking. We regularly conduct workshops, revision seminars, and inter-school math competitions to inspire confidence and academic performance.

Our Mathematics students consistently achieve top results in national examinations and gain entry into prestigious universities both locally and internationally. The school provides a supportive and focused environment with access to well-equipped classrooms, math labs, and digital resources.

Vijaya College is committed to nurturing the next generation of innovators, engineers, and researchers. We welcome passionate mathematics students who are ready to challenge themselves and excel through hard work, perseverance, and guidance.
