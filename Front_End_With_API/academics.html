Academic Programs at Vijaya College

Streams Offered

This website is specially developed for students applying to the Physical Science stream at Vijaya College.

Vijaya College offers Advanced Level (A/L) education in the following major streams:

  • Combined Mathematics Stream (Mathematics, Physics, ICT/Chemistry)
    * Students applying to the Physical Science stream must pass the school's aptitude test.
  • Science Stream (Biology, Chemistry, Physics)
    * Students applying to the Biological Science stream must pass the school's aptitude test.
  • Commerce Stream (Accounting, Business Studies, Economics)
  • Arts Stream (Sinhala, Political Science, Geography, History)
  • Technology Stream (Engineering Technology, Bio Systems, Science for Technology)

Curriculum Overview

All academic programs follow the guidelines set by the Department of Examinations of Sri Lanka, with a strong focus on practical knowledge and continuous assessment.

  • Weekly theory and lab sessions
  • Internal term exams & monthly quizzes
  • Project-based assignments and group work
  • Peer and self-evaluation strategies

Laboratories and Practical Work

We provide state-of-the-art facilities to support hands-on learning:

  • Fully equipped science laboratories (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Computer labs with modern systems and internet access
  • 3D printing, robotics, and engineering model kits for technology students

Assessment & Grading

Student performance is monitored continuously through:

  • Term exams (3 per year)
  • Class participation and assignments
  • Mock exams in preparation for A/Ls
  • Progress tracking and parent-teacher conferences

Enrichment Programs

Our students benefit from a variety of enrichment opportunities:

  • Mathematics Olympiad & Science Competitions
  • Field visits to universities, laboratories, and industries
  • Guest lectures from university professors and industry professionals
  • English language enhancement and IT workshops
